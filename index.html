About

I’m a third-year physics undergraduate at Tsinghua University working with an experimental nuclear group. My current work includes Geant4 simulations and scintillator/SiPM studies for a polarised-deuteron breakup experiment probing the nuclear symmetry energy.

I also study theoretical physics (QFT, GR) in my own time and write short technical essays. This site collects my papers, notes and demonstrations.

Outside academia I train in middle- & long-distance running and keep a running log — see the running page for details.
