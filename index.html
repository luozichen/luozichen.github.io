About & Research

I am a fourth-year Physics undergraduate at Tsinghua University. My bachelor's thesis is centred on experimental nuclear physics within Prof. Xiao Zhigang’s group, specifically the development of polarization detectors for deuteron breakup experiments.

My work combines Geant4 simulation with the hardware design and assembly of plastic scintillator arrays to probe the isovector nature of nuclear symmetry energy. Alongside this experimental focus, I maintain a strong passion for theoretical physics. This website serves as a repository for my technical notes, essays, and academic interests.
